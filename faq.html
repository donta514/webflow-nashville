How far in advance should I place an order?

We require at least 48 hours advance notice for orders, and we suggest additional lead time for larger parties. We highly recommend booking sooner than later as we cannot always guarantee availability.

Can we select multiple heat levels?

Yes! We're happy to offer multiple heat levels on your order.

Are there any peanut products used?

Our chicken is fried in peanut oil. However, we can substitute vegetable oil upon request. We cannot guarantee that peanuts haven't been used at our vendor facilities. An event coordinator can provide a full allergen list upon request.

Do you include paper products?

We do not include paper products.

Is there a minimum on deliveries?

Yes. The minimum order requirement for deliveries would be Plan A, which is eqivalent to 20 people.

I would like to order for less than 20 people.

Orders for less than 20 people can be placed with an individual store's manager with at least 48 hours notice. The restaurant accepts phone calls at 10 A.M., but in the wake of high call volume feel free to send an email.

How much are delivery fees?

Delivery fees range from $20-$50 depending on the location and how far it is from the catering kitchen. There is a $25 delivery fee for all deliveries within a 10-mile radius. Locations exceeding 10 miles can be discussed with an event coordinator.

What will your order be delivered in?

All food items will be delivered in disposable containers. Chicken, sides, and deserts will be served in standard aluminum pans.

Do you provide staff for drop services?

We do not provide staff to assist in setup or serving for our drop service.

Where are you located?

Our primary location is at the Frontier Kitchen for pickup and delivery. The address is 25387 Pleasant Valley Rd Unit 110, Chantilly, VA 20152. 20147

On Saturday’s we are located at the EatLoco Farmers Market at One Loudoun. The address is 44578 Atwater Dr, Ashburn, VA 20147.

What are your operating hours?

Catering orders may be placed Monday - Friday from 10 A.M. - 6 P.M. We are located at the EatLoco Farmer's Market in Ashburn, Va on Saturdays.

How do you accept orders?

To place an order with us you can call ahead at (202) 596-7335, or email cbj@cjsnashvillehot.com. If we don't return your email within 24 hours, please call.
