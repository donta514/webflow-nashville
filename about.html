Our Story

CJ's Nashville Hot Chicken began as a gathering of friends and family to our home. Originally from Nashville, Tennesse, we wanted to share a taste of our old home with friends in our new home in Northern Virginia.

We've served our perfected recipes to many family, friends, neighbors, and co-workers in our home for "Nashville" hot chicken parties. Now, we at CJ's Nashville Hot Chicken are happpy to share the joy of Nashville with you.
